Defining True Agentic AI

Agentic AI is NOT...

Just giving your employees Copilot or ChatGPT.

  • Requires a human to type prompts.
  • Outputs text or code (passive).
  • Acts as a highly advanced "calculator."
  • Cannot take actions in your systems independently.

Agentic AI IS...

Systems that plan, reason, and execute work.

  • Triggered by events (Webhooks, CRON jobs).
  • Outputs actions (Updates DB, sends API calls).
  • Acts as a digital "coworker."
  • Iterates on errors autonomously until the goal is met.
